Montessori Barn Toy for Babies 6-12 Months

This Montessori Barn Toy is a delightful addition to your baby's playtime, designed to nurture curiosity and early learning. It features a soft, plush farmhouse filled with six interactive sensory animals including a rabbit, chick, piglet, sheep, cow, and dog.

Each plush animal is crafted with a rattle and crinkle sound element, making it perfect for sensory exploration through touching, pinching, and shaking. This toy promotes cognitive development, fine motor skills, and visual stimulation while encouraging creative play.

The soft handle allows for easy carrying, making it ideal for on-the-go play or tummy time activities. It's built with safe, skin-friendly, and odorless materials, providing comfort and reassurance for your little one. A great choice for baby showers, birthdays, or everyday use, this toy is an excellent enrichment tool for young children.
